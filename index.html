About Us

Built on reliability

Courier Mate was founded with a simple mission: provide dependable, flexible courier services across Australia while creating great job opportunities.

01

Who We Are

Courier Mate is a courier service company connecting businesses and individuals with reliable delivery solutions. We operate across Australia, providing fast and efficient parcel delivery services.

Our team is made up of dedicated drivers and helpers who take pride in getting every delivery right.

02

What We Do

We handle deliveries of all sizes — from small parcels to larger freight. Our flexible workforce model means we can scale to meet demand while giving our team members the work-life balance they want.

Whether it's a same-day delivery or a scheduled run, Courier Mate gets it there.

03

Why Work With Us

We believe in treating our team right. That means flexible scheduling (3–7 days per week), fair pay, and a straightforward onboarding process. No red tape, no runaround. We're growing fast and always looking for reliable people to join the team.
